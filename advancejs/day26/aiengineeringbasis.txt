Presence and Frequency Penalties: Offer some control over how repetitive our output is
Presence_penalty: Number from -2 to 2
Defaults to 0 
Higher numbers increase a models likelihood
of talking about new topics

Frequency Penalties: Number from -2 to 2
Defaults to 0
At higher numbers it decreases the model's 
likelihood of repeating the exact same phrase

Fine-Tuning: Giving standard, pre-trained models a specific dataset to 
enhance it's performance.

What are the use-cases? 
Tone and style: Teach the model how you want to respond
Format: Such as JSON data
Function calling: Can be improved by fine-Tuning
Financial motivations: Reducing few shot prompt-sizes
Downgrading the model

Fine-tuning is a last resort!

Fine-tuning needs plenty of data!
- At least 50 items (more is better - within reason)
- Human-checked
